About Us.

A blog is a type of website or part of a website, which is the publication of regular articles over time in some area of personal or professional interest. By my analysis on website, it was expected that for common people to develop website, they have to face many problems and also they want to share their knowledge, technology, articles, etc. The objective of our Project is to provide all its users with easy access to relevant things and can have the information about what’s happening in the world all around. Blogs are a way to share personal information, a way for the owner to express themselves to target audience. Blog content control creation falls to the owner. People will make their blog using choose their style, template, etc. In its generic form, a blog lets a person record events and ideas freely as individual posts without a container to define their scope and to group them by different goals. Blog posts of various purposes are recorded and presented in a single linear thread. Categories may be used to separate blog posts into a hierarchical structure, but does not conceptually separate posts of different projects of the same category.
